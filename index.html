Who We Are

Founded in 2018, Kodex Creatives emerged from a simple idea: that the future of digital transformation lies at the intersection of artificial intelligence, creative excellence, and technical precision. We are a team of engineers, data scientists, designers, and marketing strategists united by a passion for innovation.

Our journey began with three founders who saw the potential of AI to revolutionize how businesses operate. Today, we've grown into a full-service agency with over 50 specialists across four countries, serving clients ranging from startups to Fortune 500 companies.

What sets us apart is our holistic approach. We don't just build technology; we build intelligent ecosystems that grow with your business. Every solution we create is designed to be scalable, adaptable, and future-proof.
