Our Story

Kodex Creatives was founded in 2025 by Lincoln Kimeu and Chris Amariati with a vision to bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and creative excellence in the African market. Starting from a shared workspace in Nairobi, we've grown into a premier digital agency serving clients across Kenya and East Africa.

Our mission is to democratize AI technology and make intelligent digital solutions accessible to businesses of all sizes. We believe that every company, regardless of scale, should have access to the tools and expertise needed to thrive in the digital economy.

Our Mission
