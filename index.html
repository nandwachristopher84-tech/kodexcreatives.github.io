Our Story

Kodex Creatives was founded in 2025 by Lincoln Kimeu and Chris Amariati with a vision to bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and creative excellence in the African market. Starting from a small office in Nairobi, we've grown into a premier digital agency serving clients across Kenya and beyond.

Our journey began with a simple observation: businesses were either getting tech solutions without creative flair or creative work without technical depth. We set out to change that by building a team that excels at both.

Today, we operate from our headquarters at Unipen Apartments in Hurlingham, where we continue to push the boundaries of what's possible with AI, creative technology, and strategic thinking.

Our Mission

To democratize AI technology and make intelligent digital solutions accessible to African businesses, empowering them to compete and thrive in the global digital economy through innovation, automation, and data-driven strategies.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading AI-powered creative agency, recognized for transforming businesses through intelligent systems that anticipate market shifts, automate complex processes, and create unprecedented value for our clients across the continent.
